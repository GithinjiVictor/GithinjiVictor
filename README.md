## 📌 Featured Projects

### 🌽 [Ushauri Soko](https://github.com/GithinjiVictor/ushauri-soko) — Market Advisory for Kenyan Farmers
> *"Ushauri Soko" means "Market Advice" in Swahili*

A data-driven decision support system helping smallholder farmers in Kenya maximize profits. Analyzes historical price trends, real-time market data, and transport costs to recommend the best markets to sell produce — replacing guesswork with data-backed decisions.

**Stack:** `Django REST Framework` · `Python` · `React.js` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Recharts` · `SQLite`

---

### 📊 [End-to-End Data Analytics Project](https://github.com/GithinjiVictor/end-to-end-data-analytics)
> LuxDev Level 3 Internship — Full ML & NLP pipeline

Covers the complete data science workflow: predictive modeling (classification), sentiment analysis (NLP), and business intelligence dashboards. Built as part of my LuxDev internship to demonstrate production-grade data engineering skills.

**Stack:** `Python` · `scikit-learn` · `NLTK` · `TextBlob` · `pandas` · `Power BI`

---

### 🌐 [BGP ISP Network Simulation](https://github.com/GithinjiVictor/LAN-WAN-Network)
> High-fidelity multi-domain ISP backbone simulation

Simulates three independent Autonomous Systems (AS 100, 200, 300) exchanging routing information via eBGP — modelling real-world inter-ISP connectivity. Developed as Founder of the Networking & Cybersecurity Lab at Mama Ngina University.

**Stack:** `Cisco Packet Tracer` · `eBGP` · `VLSM IPv4` · `BGP Neighbor Adjacencies`

---

### 📈 [Sales Analytics Dashboard — Excel](https://github.com/GithinjiVictor/Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Excel)
> Advanced Excel dashboard for a multi-regional electronics distributor

Transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights. Features automated data cleaning, KPI tracking (Revenue, Gross Profit, On-Time Delivery %), and a What-If scenario model for inflation and discount stress-testing.

**Stack:** `Microsoft Excel` · `Pivot Tables` · `Advanced Formulas` · `Slicers`

---

### 🗄️ [SQL Analytics & Data Engineering Foundations](https://github.com/GithinjiVictor/sql-analytics)
> Comprehensive PostgreSQL lab — from schema design to advanced analytics

Demonstrates proficiency in database design, window functions (`ROW_NUMBER`, `RANK`, `LEAD`, `LAG`, `NTILE`), CTEs, subqueries, and partitioned aggregations. Built as a showcase of core data engineering SQL skills.

**Stack:** `PostgreSQL` · `Window Functions` · `CTEs` · `CASE Statements`
